FAQ

Questions? We have answers

What is cuiqAnalytics?

cuiqAnalytics is a local-first analytics suite powered by AI. Everything runs on your computer or your own servers‚Äîno cloud dependency, no servers to maintain, no infrastructure complexity.

Why "local-first"?

Because it's faster, simpler, and more secure. With cuiqAnalytics: your data never leaves your environment, pipelines run 10‚Äì100√ó faster, you avoid cloud and egress costs, you have complete control, and maintenance is nearly zero.

How do teams collaborate if everything is local?

Through Git-like workflows: shared repos, version control, pull requests, optional server syncing, and AI-assisted conflict resolution. Modern collaboration without cloud services.

Is it secure?

Yes, even more secure than cloud solutions. Data never goes to external services, no third-party APIs process your information, runs 100% behind your firewall, smaller attack surface ‚Üí less risk. Local-first = security by design.

Does it scale for large workloads?

Yes. Optimized for modern hardware and vectorized processing. Scales via parallel jobs, containers, on-prem runners, and hybrid integration (process local, store anywhere). For BI, analytics, transformations, ML prototypes, and metrics‚Äîscales seamlessly.

Do I need to replace my entire stack?

No. cuiqAnalytics integrates with what you have: files, databases, data lakes, warehouses. Uses open formats (SQL, Parquet, Arrow), modular adoption with zero migration, and zero lock-in. Start with a single module.

Does it work without internet?

Yes. Everything works offline, on isolated networks, in secure or regulated environments, and on on-prem servers. Ideal for government, banking, healthcare, and enterprises with strict policies.

What use cases is it ideal for?

Data transformations (ETL/ELT), analytics and BI, governance and metrics, ML prototypes, internal training, synthetic datasets for testing. If you want speed, simplicity, and autonomy, this is for you.

What industries does it serve?

SMBs and enterprises, public sector, finance, healthcare, engineering and operations, education and training, consulting. Where cloud is expensive or slow, cuiqAnalytics shines.
